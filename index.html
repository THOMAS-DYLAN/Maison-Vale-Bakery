Our Story

A kitchen built on patience

Where every loaf
tells a story

Maison Vale was founded by Céleste Dubois in 2017 after years training in Lyon and Paris. She came home to Millbrook with flour-dusted notebooks and a conviction that a neighborhood deserved bread worth waking up for.

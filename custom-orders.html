The Process

Simple, thoughtful, personal

01

Submit an Inquiry

Fill out the form below with your event details, preferred flavors, design inspiration, and guest count. The more you share, the better we can design something that fits.

02

Consultation & Proposal

Within 2–3 business days, we'll follow up with questions, a flavor recommendation, a design direction, and a quote. For weddings, we may schedule a tasting.

03

Confirmation & Deposit

A 50% deposit holds your date. At this point your order is confirmed and we begin sourcing ingredients and planning your design.

04

Collection or Delivery

Cakes are available for pickup from our bakery. Local delivery within 15 miles is available for an additional fee. We'll coordinate the final details the week of your event.
